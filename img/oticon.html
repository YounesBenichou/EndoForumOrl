The world’s first hearing aid giving the brain the full perspective of sounds: Oticon More

Oticon More supports your brain by giving it more of what it needs to make better sense of sound. The Deep Neural Network (DNN) in Oticon More is trained with 12 million sounds from real life to recognize virtually all types of sounds to support your brain. Take better care of your brain, and your brain will take better care of you.

MoreSound Intelligence™

Scans the entire sound scene 500 times per second, resulting in a precise analysis of all sounds and the complexity of the surroundings resulting in a more natural representation of all sounds in a clear, complete, and balanced sound scene.

Better amplification without whistling

Oticon More provides optimal gain all day while eliminating the risk of whistling so you can hear better and more comfortable

Soft Speech Booster

Research proves that Oticon More users experience clearer and even better speech understanding on par with normal hearing people with Soft Speech Booster.

SmartCharger on the go!

Portable SmartCharger with a durable protective case and desktop charger for reliable recharging wherever you go.

Enjoy wireless connectivity

Oticon More offers an immersive streaming experience with excellent sound quality to your favourite devices.
